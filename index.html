Chicken Kofta Recipe

October 12, 2024 by Kayla

Chicken Kofta is a simple and flavorful dish that combines minced chicken, grated vegetables, and a touch of spice for a delicious and satisfying meal. Here's how you can make it at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of minced chicken
  • 1 large white onion, grated
  • 1 long carrot, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon of spicy chicken salt

Instructions

  1. Grate the onion and carrot, and mix them with the minced chicken, egg, flour, curry powder, and chicken salt in a large bowl.
  2. Shape the mixture into small kofta balls or cylinders.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat and cook the koftas for 6-8 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  4. Serve with rice, naan, or a salad, and enjoy!
